Services Offered

Construction Works

We provide complete, start-to-finish construction management and execution, transforming project plans into high-quality, finished structures with a steadfast commitment to safety, schedule, and budget.

Lightning Protection Systems

We provide complete, end-to-end lightning safety solutions that protect both your infrastructure and your people. Our services include the installation of robust, code-compliant lightning protection systems (LPS) to divert strike energy safely into the ground, preventing structural damage and fire. Furthermore, we integrate advanced early warning systems that detect electrostatic field changes, providing critical alerts to evacuate high-risk areas well before a strike occurs, ensuring comprehensive safety for your assets and personnel.

Advanced Materials & Solutions Supply

We provide specialized, high-tech materials and equipment for demanding projects, sourcing our products from renowned global innovators like Ingesco and OTT HydroMet. This gives you direct access to state-of-the-art lightning protection and environmental monitoring technology, ensuring certified quality, reliability, and expert technical support for your specific needs.

Infrastructure Rehabilitation

We breathe new life into aging infrastructure. Our renovation services focus on upgrading and modernizing your critical assets to improve safety, increase efficiency, and meet current regulatory standards, all while minimizing disruption to existing operations.
